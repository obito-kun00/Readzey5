Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Readezy, we value your privacy. We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a seamless reading experience:

  • Account Information: Username and email address provided during registration.
  • Reading Data: Your reading progress, bookmarks, and favorite books.
  • User Activity: Basic logs of your interactions to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly for personalized services, including:

  • Synchronizing your reading progress across devices.
  • Maintaining your library and favorites.
  • Providing customer support and security updates.

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information and uploaded manuscripts. Your password is securely hashed and never stored in plain text.

4. Third-Party Services

Readezy does not sell or trade your personal data to third parties. We may use trusted tools for site analytics and font hosting (like Google Fonts) to enhance platform performance.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time via your profile settings. If you wish to permanently remove all your data, please contact our support team.
